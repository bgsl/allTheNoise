WHAT IS ATN?

All The Noise is a boutique music publisher based in Los Angeles and New York.

Founded in 2015, All The Noise is a partnership with Sony/ATV that seeks to support unique

talent with focused attention and resources.

 

 

 

jon@allthenoi.se

 

 

 

 

ADMIN

 

All The Noise has the benefit of Sony/ATV’s administrative services as its back-end.

Our songwriters and their representatives are able to obtain administrative support in their own territory

directly from local admin staff, all of whom are experts in their field, have a passion for

the music they represent and are able to utilize their close relationships with the relevant

societies and their colleagues around the world to accurately

register songs and collect royalties.

 

Signings have access to their royalties and song data

via Sony’s SCORE portal.

 

 

 

 

 

SYNCHRONIZATION

 

All The Noise was founded with a particular eye towards synchronization,

balancing personal relationships with music supervisors in Los Angeles with the global

reach of Sony/ATV’s department of over 100 people.

 

 

Music Publishing Services

 

 

 

All The Noise is a full service music publisher—a boutique setup with the reach and technological muscle of a major. Backed by cutting edge SCORE technology, ATN administers the copyrights of a select group of clients, effectively and globally tracking performance royalties, digital performance royalties, synchronization royalties, mechanical royalties, digital mechanical royalties, and print royalties. Everything can be tracked in the digital age, so ATN keeps writers up speed with timely, accurate accounting from all of the sources of income their music derives. ATN also offers full creative support, in both A&R and sync, with a focused team that helps present creative and business opportunities of all sorts.

 

 

 

All The Noise is proud to feature writers Khalid, DJDS, Clams Casino, and Cousin Stizz.

Collecting Royalties and Fees on Your Behalf

 

 

 

If you feel that you may have missing royalties, All The Noise can assist. Through partnership with Paperchain.io, ATN has taken steps towards the future of royalty collection and tracking as it works to solve the problem of unclaimed compositional royalties and masters and compositions that have been improperly matched.

 

 

Copyright Management

 

 

Using a SCORE’s cutting edge copyright management technology and a combination of proprietary cataloging and delivery tools, All The Noise administers the catalogs of artists with an eye towards the digital age, with deep relationships to all of the digital service providers. ATN’s catalog management tools all writers and their teams to get a clear picture of their music’s publishing value and the all of a song’s various income.
